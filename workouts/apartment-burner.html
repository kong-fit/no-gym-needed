The Apartment
Burner

A quiet, no-jumping full-body circuit built for thin floors and downstairs neighbors. Every move keeps at least one foot on the ground — you'll still be breathing hard by minute eight.

6
Exercises
3

0

The circuit

Do all 6 moves back to back, rest 60 seconds, repeat for 3 rounds total.

01

Reverse Lunge to Knee Drive

Step back into a lunge, then drive the back knee up to hip height as you stand. Builds leg strength and balance without any impact.

Keep your front heel planted the whole time — don't let it lift.

10 / side
No rest between sides
02

Slow Push-Up

A standard push-up, but take 3 full seconds to lower and 1 second to press up. Slowing the tempo replaces the need for extra reps or weight.

Elbows at 45°, not flared out to the sides.

8 reps
Knees down if needed
03

Standing Bicycle Crunch

Standing tall, drive opposite elbow to opposite knee, controlled and slow. Trains rotational core strength with zero floor noise.

Twist from your ribs, not your arms.

16 total
8 per side
04

Wall Sit

Back flat against a wall, thighs parallel to the floor. A silent way to torch your quads — no impact, no noise, nowhere to hide.

Press your lower back into the wall the entire time.

30 sec hold
—
05

Glute Bridge March

From a bridge position, lift one foot at a time without letting your hips drop. Quiet, floor-based, and brutal on the glutes by rep 10.

Hips stay level — imagine a cup of water balanced on your stomach.

20 total
10 per side
06

Forearm Plank

Standard forearm plank, holding a straight line from shoulders to heels. Closes every round with full-body tension.

Squeeze your glutes — it protects your lower back and makes the hold easier.

30 sec hold
Rest 60 sec, repeat circuit

Make it harder or easier

Same circuit, scaled to where you actually are today.

Wall Sit, scaled

Easier — Hold for 15 seconds instead of 30, or rest your hands on your thighs.
Standard — 30-second hold, arms crossed over your chest.
Harder — Hold for 45 seconds, or lift one foot for the last 10 seconds.
