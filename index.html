How this actually works

No app to download. No account required to start.

01

Measure your space

Most workouts here fit in 6×6 feet — about the size of a yoga mat with room to swing your arms. Clear it once and you're set.

02

Pick by time, not mood

Every workout is tagged by exact minutes required. Eight minutes before a call still counts as a workout.

03

Progress without weights

Each plan includes a harder variation, so the workout grows with you instead of needing a heavier dumbbell.
